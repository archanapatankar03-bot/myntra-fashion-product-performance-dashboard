# 👗 Myntra Fashion Product Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Data Analytics solution developed using **Python, MySQL, and Power BI**. The objective is to analyze Myntra fashion product data to understand brand performance, customer engagement, product categories, pricing, and discount strategies.

The project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow from raw data cleaning to interactive dashboard creation and business insight generation.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Fashion retailers need to answer important business questions such as:

- Which brands receive the highest customer engagement?
- Which product categories have the largest product catalog?
- How do discounts relate to customer ratings?
- Which products have the highest customer reviews?
- How can business users make better merchandising decisions using data?

This dashboard helps answer these questions through interactive visualizations.

## 🧹 Data Cleaning Performed

- Checked and handled missing values
- Converted data types
- Removed duplicate records
- Filled missing ratings using median values
- Filled missing reviews with zero
- Replaced missing discount prices using original prices
- Cleaned and prepared data for analysis

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Roadster receives the highest customer engagement based on customer reviews.
- Indian Wear has the largest product catalog.
- Average customer ratings indicate high product satisfaction.
- Higher discounts do not necessarily lead to higher customer ratings.
- Women's fashion products represent the largest portion of the product catalog.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-performing brands.
- Improve visibility of low-engagement categories.
- Encourage customers to leave product reviews.
- Evaluate discount strategies to maximize profitability.
- Continue maintaining high product quality.
